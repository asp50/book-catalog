📥 Restore / Transfer to Another Device

To move your catalog to an iPad (or any other device): tap Export Full Catalog above, AirDrop or iCloud the CSV to the other device, open the app there, then import it here.

Merge adds new books and skips any ISBNs already in the catalog. Replace wipes the current catalog first — use this for a clean transfer to a fresh device.
